Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

1. Information We Collect

We may collect:

a. Personal Information (provided voluntarily)

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • School or academic details (if applying to programs)

b. Non‑Personal Information

  • Browser type
  • Device type
  • Pages visited
  • General location (city/country)

This data helps us improve our website and programs.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Respond to inquiries
  • Process program applications
  • Communicate updates or opportunities
  • Improve our website and services
  • Maintain safety and program integrity

We do not sell or rent your personal information.

3. Cookies & Tracking

Our website may use cookies to improve user experience, analyze site traffic, and remember preferences. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

4. Sharing Your Information

We may share information only when necessary:

  • With trusted service providers (e.g., email platforms)
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect the safety of students and staff

We never share information for advertising or commercial purposes.

5. Data Protection

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no online system is completely secure.

6. Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental or guardian consent. If we learn that such information was collected unintentionally, we will delete it promptly.

7. International Users

Our programs operate in Cameroon, but our website may be accessed globally. By using our site, you consent to the transfer and processing of your information in accordance with this policy.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to your data
  • Request corrections or deletion
  • Opt out of communications
  • Withdraw consent

To exercise these rights, contact us at info@carolsprepacademy.org.

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised “Last Updated” date.

10. Contact Us

For questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, contact: info@carolsprepacademy.org
